ABOUT US

Steak houses started in London in the 1690s and served individual portions of meat, known as chops.The traditional nature of the food served was zealously maintained through the later 19th century despite the new cooking styles from the Continent, which were becoming fashionable. The houses were normally only open for men. The steakhouse started in the United States in the mid-19th century as a development from traditional inns and bars.Steakhouses can be casual or formal fine dining restaurants.
